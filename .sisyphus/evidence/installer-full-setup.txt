```yaml
    depends_on:

        condition: service_healthy
  
  nginx:
    depends_on:
      - conduit  # or synapse
      - certbot
  
  certbot:
    depends_on:
      - nginx  # For ACME challenges
```

**Startup Order:**
1. postgres (no deps)
2. conduit/synapse (after postgres healthy)
3. certbot (after nginx)
4. nginx (after backend)
5. coturn (independent)
