# Privacy Policy for ArmorChat

**Last Updated:** February 25, 2026

---

## Introduction

ArmorChat ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our encrypted messaging application.

**Key Principle:** ArmorChat is designed with privacy-first architecture. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted, meaning we cannot read, access, or share the content of your communications.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Username/Display Name:** For identification within the app
- **Email Address:** For account verification and support (if provided)
- **Device Identifier:** For app stability and security purposes

### Message Data
- **Message Content:** End-to-end encrypted. We cannot access or read your messages.
- **Metadata:** Message timestamps and recipient identifiers (encrypted in transit)

### Technical Data
- **Crash Reports:** Anonymous crash data via Sentry to improve app stability
- **Analytics:** Limited usage analytics via Firebase Analytics (aggregate, non-personal)
- **Device Info:** OS version, app version, device type for compatibility

---

## How We Use Information

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Account Info | Account creation, authentication, support |
| Encrypted Messages | Delivery to intended recipients only |
| Crash Reports | Bug fixing and app stability improvement |
| Analytics | Feature usage patterns (anonymized) |

---

## Data Security

### Encryption Standards
- **Messages:** AES-256-GCM encryption with ECDH key exchange
- **Database:** SQLCipher with 256-bit passphrase
- **Transport:** TLS 1.3 with certificate pinning
- **Keys:** Stored in AndroidKeyStore (hardware-backed where available)

### Security Features
- Biometric authentication for app access
- Secure clipboard with automatic clearing
- Session timeout and auto-lock
- Zero-knowledge architecture

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your messages with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Allow third-party trackers in the app

We **MAY** disclose information if:
- Required by law (with valid legal process)
- Necessary to protect our rights or user safety
- With your explicit consent

---

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while account is active; deleted upon request
- **Messages:** Stored on your device only; encrypted on server during delivery
- **Crash Reports:** Automatically deleted after 90 days
- **Analytics:** Aggregated and anonymized; no individual retention
